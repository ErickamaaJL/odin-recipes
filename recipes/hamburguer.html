Hamburguer Recipe

Description

Simple recipe on how to prepare a hamburger

Ingredients

  • Hamburger bun
  • Breadcrumbs
  • Eggs
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Lettuce
  • Yellow cheese
  • Ground beef
  • Sauce
  • Onion

Steps

  1. Prepare the ingredients that we are going to need for the preparation of the hamburger.
  2. Preheat the grill to high temperature and lightly oil the grate.
  3. Whisk egg, salt and pepper in a medium bowl.
  4. Add the ground beef and bread crumbs and mix with your hands until well blended.
  5. Form the hamburgers.
  6. Place the burgers on the grill, cover and cook for 6 to 8 minutes per side.
  7. Cut the hamburger bun in half and place the bottom part of the bun, the lettuce, the meat, the cheese, the onion, the sauce and finally the other part of the bun.
