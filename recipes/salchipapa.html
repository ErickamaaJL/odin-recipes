Salchipapa Recipe

Definition

In this recipe you will learn how to make salchipapas.

Ingredients

  • Sausages
  • Potatoes
  • Oil
  • Salt
  • Mustard
  • Tomato sauce
  • Mayonnaise

Steps

  1. Peel the potatoes, wash them and cut them into sticks.
  2. Cut the sausages, either into slices or diagonally.
  3. In a pan, add oil and heat it over medium heat.
  4. Add the potatoes.
  5. After 15 minutes, turn up the heat and cook for 5 more minutes.
  6. After 20 minutes of cooking, add the chopped sausages.
  7. Continue cooking for 5 more minutes.
  8. Remove the potatoes and sausages from the pan and leave them on a tray with absorbent paper.
  9. Add salt and pepper to taste.
  10. Once the excess oil has been removed, we proceed to plate, putting the french fries as a base and the sausages on top of them.
  11. Add the sauces to our liking.
